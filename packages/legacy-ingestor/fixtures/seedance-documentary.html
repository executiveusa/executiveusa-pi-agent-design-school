Urban Documentary: Commute at Sunrise

A fly-on-the-wall observational documentary capturing the quiet poetry of urban commuters at dawn. Shot type: Wide establishing shot of a subway platform gradually filling with people, then tight close-ups of individual faces reflecting in train windows. Camera: Steady handheld, moving through the crowd with naturalistic motion. Lighting: Cool pre-dawn blue hour transitioning to warm early morning sunlight streaming through platform skylights. Mood: Introspective, meditative, finding beauty in routine and human connection amid urban solitude. Subject: Diverse commuters, their expressions, a dropped coffee cup, reflected faces in glass, crowded train cars, the rhythm of the city waking up.

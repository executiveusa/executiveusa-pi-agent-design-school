# CloneyFanz — Consent and Identity Protection (Planning Only)

**No implementation permitted.**

## Requirements

- No real-person impersonation under any circumstances
- All AI-generated likenesses require explicit consent from the depicted person
- Creators must verify their identity and consent to all AI tools used with their likeness
- No fan-generated AI content using creator likenesses without creator opt-in

## Identity Verification
- Creator identity must be verified against government ID
- Likeness consent must be documented and retained
- Consent revocation process must be immediate and complete

## Non-Negotiable
- Zero-tolerance policy for non-consensual intimate imagery (NCII)
- Immediate takedown procedures for reported NCII
- Law enforcement cooperation protocol
